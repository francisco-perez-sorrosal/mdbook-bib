# Computing Foundations

## Information Theory

Claude Shannon's groundbreaking work {{#cite shannon1948}} laid the foundation for modern information theory and digital communications.

## Document Preparation

Donald Knuth developed TeX {{#cite knuth1984}}, which revolutionized mathematical typesetting. Building on this work, Leslie Lamport created LaTeX {{#cite lamport1986}} to make document preparation more accessible.

## Theoretical Computing

Alan Turing's seminal paper {{#cite turing1950}} on computing machinery and intelligence posed fundamental questions about artificial intelligence that remain relevant today.
